HueRush ("the Game") is developed and published by Kamil Svoboda. This Privacy Policy explains how the Game collects, uses, and protects user data. By downloading or playing HueRush, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information Collected

  • Nickname: A player-generated name entered within the Game.
  • Score: The numerical result of gameplay.
  • Advertising Identifiers: Such as Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA, used for delivering rewarded ads.
  • Device Information: May include device type, operating system, and usage statistics (collected anonymously via third-party services).

No other personal data (e.g., email, IP address, location) is collected or stored by the Developer.

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Leaderboard Functionality: Nicknames and scores are submitted to Unity Leaderboard services and displayed publicly within the Game.
  • Rewarded Advertising: Advertising identifiers are used by third-party ad networks to deliver ads and track performance.
  • Gameplay Analytics: Anonymous usage data may be used to improve game performance and user experience.

3. Public Display Notice

Nicknames and scores are visible to all players via the in-game leaderboard. Players are advised not to use real names or personally identifiable information when creating their nickname.

4. Third-Party Services

HueRush integrates the following third-party services:

  • Unity Leaderboards: For score tracking and ranking.
  • Unity Ads / Google AdMob: For rewarded advertisements.
  • Unity Analytics (if enabled): For anonymous gameplay statistics.

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Unity Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

The Developer does not control or access personal data processed by these services.

5. Children‚Äôs Privacy

HueRush is suitable for all age groups. The Game does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The nickname submitted is anonymous and publicly visible. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted identifiable personal data, please contact the Developer to request removal.

For compliance with COPPA and GDPR, HueRush does not collect, store, or share personal data that could identify a child.

6. Data Storage and Security

HueRush does not store any user data on its own servers. All data is processed and stored by third-party services. The Developer does not retain, sell, or share any user data.

Security measures are in place via Unity and Google to protect data from unauthorized access.

7. User Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Request access to any data submitted.
  • Request deletion of data (via Unity or Google).
  • Withdraw consent for advertising personalization (via device settings).

The Developer does not directly process or store personal data and cannot fulfill such requests directly, but will assist where possible.

8. Opt-Out Options

Users may opt out of personalized ads by adjusting device settings:

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track

Note: Opting out may affect the availability or relevance of rewarded ads.
